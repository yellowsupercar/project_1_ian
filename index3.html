How to check how much ping you have

There are alot of options in games to find your ping, But you can just look up a ping test to see what your ms is.If you can't find it there, here is another step by step guide on how to find your ping on windows.

  1. Open your start key on the bottom left and type in "cmd"
  2. Next, open cmd and type in "ping 104.160.131.3 -t"
